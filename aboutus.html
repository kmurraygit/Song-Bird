About Us

Mission

Song Bird Music empowers the youth of Cedar City through music
education, academic support, and mentorship. Our goal is to
provide our kids with the resources they need to lead positive,
productive and self-reliant lives.

Founders

Katie Murray
Allyson Cole

Program Objectives

To instill a love and passion for music.
To help children build their self-esteem.
To help children build self confidence.
To help children succeed in school.
To help children realize their full potential.

Measures of Success

Number of students enrolled
The program's retention rates
Total hours of instruction
Student GPA and GPA improvement
Student intrument skill level and rate of improvement
